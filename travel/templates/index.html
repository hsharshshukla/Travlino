Welcome to India

India, the land of Mystery, Diversity and Peace

Indian Culture

Indian Culture is known for its inclusivity. Inspite of different Religions and ethnicities it's still united. People of different Religion have been living in Peace and Harmony for centuries.

Indian Food

India is known for its Spicey food across the globe.Indian tastes include indeginious dishes as well as those influenced by Greek,Arab, and Roman Flavours due to the trade relations from Ancient times. Most famous dishes include Chicken Tikka Masala, Indian Curry, Chutney's, Indian Sweets ...

Indian Festivals

India is a land of Celebrations with festivals like Diwali,known for victory of Good over Evil, Holi, festival of Colors and also regional ones like Lohdi,Onam.

Travel India

There are a lot of places in India which have mark in history. The best of all is Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders in the world, true symbol of Love. Royal Palaces in Rajasthan reminiscent of the Royal heritage of the Rajput Clans. Monuments in India are vastly influenced by Mughal Architecture due to presence of Mughal emperors. Few famous monuments are Lal Qila, Qutub Minar, Humayun Maqbara. In Amritsar, Golden Temple, one of the biggest religious places of Sikhism. Religious cities Haridwar and Varanasi, have many mysteries of Hinduism. Nalanda University,one of the Oldest University in the world. Pushkar, known around the world, according to legends,Lord Brahma, believed to be Creator of Universe dropped a lotus to the ground leading to immediate creation of lake, hence named Pushkar. It is the ultimate pilgrimage for Hindus that must be undertaken to attain salvation. Goa known for its Beaches across the globe. Rameshwaram one of the most famous temples in Hinduism. Cheraman Juma Masjid, world's Second mosque was built in Kerala India.
